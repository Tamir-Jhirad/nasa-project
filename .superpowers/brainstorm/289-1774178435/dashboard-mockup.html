Data Science Methodology
Impact Hazard Score
Score = log₁₀( (Mass × Velocity) / Distance² + 1 )
  • Mass — spherical body, ρ = 2,000 kg/m³
  • Velocity — relative velocity at approach (m/s)
  • Distance — nominal miss distance (m)
  • log₁₀ — prevents extreme values dominating
Diameter Estimation
D = (1329 / √p) × 10^(−H/5) [km]
  • H — absolute magnitude (NASA catalog)
  • p = 0.14 — assumed geometric albedo
  • Used for ~99% of objects (NASA omits diameter)
